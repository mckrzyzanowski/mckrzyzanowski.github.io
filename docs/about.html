Project Experience

PhenX Toolkit

I have worn multiple hats for the PhenX (consensus measures for Phenotypes and eXposures) project. I currently serve as the Toolkit Lead, managing releases of new content and data, visioning new tools and features, presenting updates to the project PIs and project scientists at the funding agency, and coordinating a team of developers, data engineers, and file processors. In addition, I have previously served as a Working Group (WG) Supervisor, serving to work with funding agency project scientists, recruiting and managing WG members, ensuring the scope of the project is aligned, and seeing through completion of the WG goals.
